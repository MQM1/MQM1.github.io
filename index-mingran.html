My Bio

I know Game design, Animation, Audio, Storytelling and now.. Data and Information Visualization.

My name is Megan Nyre, and I have a variety of interests beyond my area of concentration. One of my interests is helping people understand information that is presented to them. After five years of working in industry as an Industrial Engineer and LSS Black Belt, one of the many struggles I faced was finding a way to share the information I found with the managers and workers around me.

By taking this class in Data Visualization, I hope to not only find better ways to share information, but have the technical experience to enhance those visualizations to be truly interesting.

My Experience

I am currently pursuing my PhD in Computer Graphics Technology at Purdue University. My Research direction is Data and Information Visualization. Also, I have completed my Masters in Digital Arts and Science from the University of Florida. I have always aspired to be a contributor at Twitter. I believe that my background and passion for inspiring others satisfies the qualifications for this internship.
